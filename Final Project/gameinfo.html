Your goal is too kill as many slimes as possible

Attack increases your damage to the slimes
Defense reduces the damage you take from the slimes
Speed lets you attack first if it is higher than the slimes
Health increases the amount of hits you can take

Each level of slime increases all its stats by 1 and increases its health by 2
Every lvl of slimes gives 50 more exp and 10 more gold
